Previously in the game, the player woke up in a stasis pod, and was helped out of it by Eliza. She helped the player escape an emergency situation on the stasis pod deck, guiding them to the safety of the control room. Eliza then helped the player find their way to the air lock, with the aim of reaching the Bio Hab. But this requires a short spacewalk. During the preceding game play, the player has got to know Eliza a little. They are starting to develop a rapport.
